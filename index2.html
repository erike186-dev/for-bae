For Phoo

My Love Feels Like A Movie

Every scene in my heart belongs to you

If love could become a sky, it would be this pink evening. If love could become a story, it would be the story of me finding Phoo and never wanting the scene to end.

Phoo, loving you feels like standing inside the most beautiful movie ever made, where every frame glows softer because you are in it. Before you, days could pass like ordinary pages. But after you, everything became more alive. The air feels warmer. Music sounds deeper. Even silence feels meaningful, because my heart keeps filling it with your name.

Sometimes I try to explain what you mean to me, and words feel too small for it. You are not just someone I love. You are the calm after my storms, the light that reaches me even on the darkest nights, the person who makes my world feel gentle and full of color. You make my life feel less like survival and more like poetry.

When I imagine the future, I do not just see time passing. I see moments with you. I see your smile becoming my favorite sunrise. I see your voice becoming the song I want to hear after every hard day. I see a life where love is not something I search for anymore, because I already found it in you. And that is why my heart keeps choosing you, again and again, like the hero in a love story who already knows exactly where home is.

If the world ever asked me what my greatest blessing is, I would say your name without even thinking. Because Phoo, you are the dream I never want to wake up from, the chapter I never want to close, and the forever I want to keep loving with all that I am.

Pink sky, mountain dreams, and love flying everywhere for Phoo.
